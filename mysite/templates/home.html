More About Sign Language

Sign language detection is an innovative technology that aims to bridge the communication gap between individuals who use sign language and those who don't understand it. It involves the use of computer vision and machine learning techniques to interpret and understand sign language gestures and convert them into written or spoken language.

Sign language is a visual and gestural form of communication used primarily by individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing. It relies on a combination of hand movements, facial expressions, and body language to convey meaning. However, for individuals who do not understand sign language, this form of communication can be a barrier to effective interaction.

Sign language detection technology utilizes various algorithms and models to analyze video or image inputs and identify different sign language gestures. It can recognize specific hand shapes, movements, and facial expressions associated with different signs. By detecting and interpreting these gestures, the technology can convert sign language into text or speech, enabling communication between sign language users and non-signers.
